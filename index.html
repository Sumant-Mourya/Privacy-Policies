Privacy Policy for Dialer

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Introduction

Dialer ("we", "our", or "the app") is a phone dialer application developed by Amigo. This Privacy Policy explains how information is used and protected when you use our app.

Dialer processes most data locally on your device. Some limited data may be collected by third-party services such as Google AdMob, as described below.

Fake Call Feature

The Fake Call feature allows you to simulate an incoming call for personal use. Fake calls are generated locally on your device and do not contact real phone numbers, emergency services, or external systems.

This feature is clearly labeled within the app and is intended for entertainment or privacy purposes only.

Information We Collect

Dialer operates primarily as an offline application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers.

The app accesses the following data locally on your device:

  • Contacts – To display contact names, phone numbers, and photos
  • Call Logs – To show your recent call history
  • Phone State – To manage incoming and outgoing calls
  • SMS – To send auto-reply and quick reply messages when enabled

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or to any third party by the app itself.

Microphone & Audio Usage

Dialer requires microphone access to enable live call audio routing during phone calls. The microphone is used only while you are actively on a call.

Dialer does not record, store, process, or transmit any audio. All call audio is handled directly by the Android system and your device hardware.

No call audio is saved locally or shared with any server or third party.

Third-Party Services

Dialer uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information as described in Google’s Privacy Policy, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

This data collection is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob.

Ad Display Policy

Advertisements are never shown during active phone calls, incoming call screens, or over the lock screen.

Ads appear only when you voluntarily access ad-gated features within app settings or configuration screens.

Permissions

  • Phone & Call – Make, receive, and manage phone calls
  • Contacts – Display caller and contact information
  • Call Log – Show recent call history
  • Notifications – Incoming and missed call alerts
  • SMS – Auto-reply and quick reply messages
  • Bluetooth – Headset audio routing
  • Do Not Disturb – Silent All Calls feature
  • Storage / Media – Select custom call backgrounds
  • Internet – Load advertisements
  • Alarm – Schedule fake calls

Data Storage

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device, including:

  • Call background preferences
  • Sleep control schedules
  • Call blocking rules
  • Quick reply templates
  • Theme settings
  • Ad gate cooldown and skip counters

You can clear all app data at any time via Settings → Apps → Dialer → Clear Data.

Advertisements

Dialer uses Google AdMob rewarded ads to support development. Ads are shown only when you choose to unlock certain premium features.

If ads fail to load, features are unlocked after multiple attempts. We do not show intrusive or forced ads.

Children’s Privacy

Dialer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored on your device. Since all app data remains local, security depends on your device’s system protections such as lock screen and encryption.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated “Last updated” date.
